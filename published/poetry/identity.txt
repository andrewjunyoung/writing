Revisiting that garden
where i spent my youth
reminded me of what it was like
and what i was like,
and made me realize
those days
are totally gone

-- identity

その日本庭
日本で過ごした日々を思い出した。
でもその日々
帰らざると
分かった。

—— アイデンティティ

Written at a cafe in downtown Portland, OR, following a visit to a Japanese garden.
